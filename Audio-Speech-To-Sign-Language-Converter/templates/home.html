About The Project

This system is designed to bridge the communication gap between hearing & speech-impaired people and those who do not understand Indian Sign Language (ISL). Using cutting-edge AI and 3D character animations, we make communication seamless and inclusive.

How It Works

ISL ➜ Text ➜ Speech

For deaf/mute persons to express themselves:

  • Camera captures hand gestures (ISL)
  • AI recognizes gestures in real-time
  • AI recognizes them and converts to text
  • Text is converted to audible speech

Speech/Text ➜ ISL

For hearing persons to communicate back:

  • Hearing person speaks or types a sentence
  • System converts speech to text
  • Maps text to specific ISL gestures
  • Displays 3D ISL animations/videos
